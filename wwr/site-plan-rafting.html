Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide greater outreach to audiences beyond word of mouth. This is to create more revenue for the company and to provide information regarding location, pricing, and availability for white water rafting in our area.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers include teenagers and young adults, ages 16-35. These individuals would be seeking some 'high adventure' opportunities and likely have less to no children, thus providing greater expendable income for these experiences. What do they want? Customers want to enjoy nature through a high adventure experience that can be enjoyed repeatedly with friends and family. How will my audience usually access my site? Because of the younger nature of the target audience, most customers will access the site through mobile device.
